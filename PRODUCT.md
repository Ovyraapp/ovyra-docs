# PRODUCT

## Overview

Ovyra is an AI-powered city discovery platform that helps people discover where to go, what to do, and what fits their taste right now. It sits between search, maps, local media, and concierge-style recommendation, but aims to feel faster, smarter, and more personalized than any one of those categories.

## Core Problem

Urban discovery is fragmented. Users bounce between maps, social content, event listings, articles, and friend recommendations. The result is choice overload, stale information, and poor trust in generic ranking systems.

## Product Thesis

If Ovyra can combine live city context, semantic understanding, and personal taste signals, discovery becomes more useful and more habit-forming. The product should answer not only "what is nearby" but "what fits me now".

## Primary Users

- curious locals looking for new places and experiences
- travelers who want context-rich city guidance
- socially active users planning nights, weekends, and meetups
- taste-driven users who want recommendations aligned with vibe, mood, and intent

## MVP Scope

- AI-assisted onboarding for preference capture
- personalized feed of places and events
- conversational and filter-based discovery
- maps with contextual recommendation layers
- saved collections and lightweight feedback loops

## Success Metrics

- onboarding completion rate
- first recommendation save rate
- weekly returning discovery sessions
- recommendation click-through and save depth
- downstream signals of trust such as repeated use in the same city
