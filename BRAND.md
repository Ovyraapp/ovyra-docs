# BRAND

## Positioning

Ovyra should feel like the premium intelligence layer for city life. The product is not playful tourism software and not a crowded local directory. It should communicate focus, clarity, and technical sophistication.

## Style Attributes

- futuristic
- premium
- minimalist
- dark mode first
- intelligent, not noisy
- confident, not corporate

## Visual Direction

- primary color: `#3B82F6`
- base background: `#0F1115`
- favor deep blacks, slate neutrals, and crisp white typography
- use restrained gradients and subtle glows rather than loud neon
- motion should feel smooth and intentional, never decorative for its own sake

## Inspiration References

- OpenAI for clarity and composure
- Linear for precision and product discipline
- Vercel for modern developer polish
- Arc Browser for premium futurism
- Perplexity for information-forward confidence

## Voice

- concise
- intelligent
- calm
- premium
- useful before promotional

## Design Rules

- avoid clutter and oversized navigation
- prioritize content hierarchy over ornament
- make recommendations feel curated, not dumped from a feed
- preserve strong spacing and rhythm in dark mode
