# CONTENT STRATEGY

## Objective

Content in Ovyra should improve discovery quality, trust, and growth. It is not filler. It should make the platform feel current, local, and taste-aware.

## Content Layers

- structured listings for places and events
- AI-enriched summaries and tags
- editorial collections such as neighborhood guides, date-night picks, and seasonal lists
- product marketing content that clarifies the discovery value proposition

## Early Distribution Strategy

- start with one or two flagship cities instead of shallow global coverage
- create high-signal editorial collections that demonstrate taste
- use content to improve cold-start recommendations before behavioral data matures
- turn standout recommendations into shareable landing pages and social assets

## Editorial Principles

- useful first, clever second
- local specificity beats generic travel language
- avoid hype-driven phrasing that reduces trust
- maintain a premium and concise tone

## AI and Human Workflow

- AI can draft summaries, tag entities, and cluster themes
- human review should shape flagship collections and homepage-quality surfaces
- preserve source provenance where possible for trust and operations

## Metrics

- save rate by content collection
- conversion from content pages into recommendation interactions
- search entry rate into curated city content
- repeat usage of editorial surfaces by returning users
